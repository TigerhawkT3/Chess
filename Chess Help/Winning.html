Winning

Playing the Game ››

In chess, a player loses when there are no moves they can make that would prevent their opponent from winning on their next turn. This situation is known as checkmate. In Chess, you win the game by actually capturing the enemy king. At this point, you may review the game (see Navigation), start a new game (see New), or open a previously-saved game (see Open...).
