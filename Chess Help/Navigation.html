Navigation

Menu Options ››

The Navigation menu allows you to step back and forth through a game's existing moves. Made a bad move? Step backwards. Your opponent didn't want you to do that? Step forwards. Looking at an old saved game? Step to the beginning of the match. Can't wait to see how that match ends? Step straight to the end.


If you step back so that you are no longer at the most current move of the match, making a manual move at that point will wipe out all moves ahead of it. If you want to be able to get back to that series of moves, use the Save As... function before making a manual move.
