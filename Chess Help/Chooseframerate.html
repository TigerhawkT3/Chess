Choose framerate...

Menu Options ›› Settings ›› Board ›› Background ››

To choose a framerate for your image sequence or animation, choose Settings > Board > Background > Choose framerate..., type in a framerate in frames per second, then click OK or press Enter. 30 frames per second is a common framerate. Floating-point values such as 0.5 (one frame every two seconds) are acceptable.
