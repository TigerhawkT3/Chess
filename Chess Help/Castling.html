Castling

Playing the Game ››

Castling moves the king behind other pieces in a defensive maneuver, but this is only available under certain conditions.


  • The king must not have moved yet.
  • A rook that has not moved yet participates.
  • There must be no pieces between the king and the participating rook.
  • It must be your turn.


When castling, the participating rook moves as far towards the king as it can, and then the king jumps over the participating rook, landing right next to it.


To castle, use the Castle menu.
