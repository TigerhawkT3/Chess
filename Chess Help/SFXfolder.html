SFX folder...

Menu Options ›› Settings ›› Audio ››

To choose a new folder of sound effects, choose Settings > Audio > SFX folder... or press Ctrl+U, browse to the folder you'd like to use, and click OK.


This folder must contain the following files:


  • audio_on.ogg
  • castle.ogg
  • computer_move.ogg
  • explosion.ogg
  • game_start.ogg
  • move_piece.ogg
  • select_piece.ogg
  • torpedo.ogg
  • ui_toggle.ogg
  • undo.ogg


Support is limited to OGG files, as other formats (like MP3) may cause crashes.
