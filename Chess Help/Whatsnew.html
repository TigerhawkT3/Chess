What's new

Introduction ››

Chess 10 has many new features:


  • Volume control system that allows you to customize the volume of each of the game's sounds
  • Time control function with a variety of timers
  • Background image selection - a wallpaper for your match
  • Background animation selection - an animated wallpaper for your match
  • Grid line color can now be customized
  • Powerful developer console


Chess 10 is only compatible with save files from version 9 or later. The old version lists the location of each piece, while the new method's save files contain a series of moves.
