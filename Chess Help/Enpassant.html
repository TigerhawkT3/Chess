En passant

Playing the Game ››

If a pawn – Black, for example – takes its first move, moves two spaces, and lands next to a White pawn, that White pawn may move to the square that the Black pawn skipped and capture the Black pawn as if the Black pawn had moved only one square. This is known as an en passant capture. If an en passant capture is available, it will be highlighted like a normal move.
