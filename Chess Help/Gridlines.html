Gridlines...

Menu Options ›› Settings ›› Board ››

To choose a new color for the board's grid lines, choose Settings > Board > Grid lines..., choose a color, and click OK.


This option colors the board's grid lines in the shade of your choosing. You can also remove the grid lines (turn them transparent) by clicking Cancel instead of OK.
