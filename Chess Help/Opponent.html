Opponent

Menu Options ›› Settings ››

The Opponent menu allows you to change the current opponent, with the following options:


Easy AI: An easy chess AI will move for Black.

Hard AI: A hard chess AI will move for Black.

Human: A human player can control Black's pieces.


You can change the opponent at any time. The game checks the opponent mode after every White move and acts accordingly.
